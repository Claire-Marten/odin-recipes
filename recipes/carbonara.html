Carbonara

Description

This is Carbonara. It does NOT include cream, ffs.

Ingredients

  • spaghetti
  • egg
  • pepper
  • pecorino cheese
  • bacon, or guancale
  • garlic
  • oil

Steps

  1. start cooking the pasta
  2. render down the protein
  3. add the garlic to the protein
  4. separately, combine egg yolk, pepper, cheese
  5. save some pasta water and drain the pasta well
  6. add the pasta to the protein and oil, soak up the oil
  7. add the egg mixture and mix, the pan should be off the heat
  8. add pasta water to loosen
