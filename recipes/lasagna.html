Lasagna

Description

This is a picture of lasagna, my favourite pasta dish!

Ingredients

  • pasta sheets
  • tomato paste
  • tomatos (diced)
  • minced beef
  • minced pork
  • salt and pepper
  • celery
  • carrot
  • garlic
  • onion
  • cheese
  • milk
  • flour
  • butter

Steps

  1. Brown off the beef and pork
  2. Cook down the vegetables
  3. Add diced tomatoes, tomato paste
  4. Separately, make a bechamel sauce
  5. When both are ready, layer the following:
  6. Very thin later of meat sauce
  7. Pasta sheet
  8. Meat sauce
  9. Bechamel
  10. Cook in the oven
